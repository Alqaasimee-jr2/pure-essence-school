# 🚀 Pure Essence School — Launch & Domain Guide

This guide explains how to move from the current `.vercel.app` staging link to a professional school domain (e.g., `pureessenceschool.edu.ng` or `.com.ng`).

---

## 1. Domain Registration
For a Nigerian school, we recommend a `.edu.ng` (Educational) or `.com.ng` (Commercial) domain.

### **Where to register?**
- **Whogohost**, **Gigalayer**, or **DomainKing** are reliable local registrars for `.ng` domains.
- **Requirements for .edu.ng**: You may need to provide a scan of the school's registration certificate (CAC or Ministry of Education approval).

---

## 2. Connecting to Vercel
Once you have the domain, follow these steps to link it to the website:

1.  **Log in to Vercel**: Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) and select the `pure-essence-school` project.
2.  **Add Domain**: 
    - Settings > Domains.
    - Type your new domain (e.g., `pureessenceschool.edu.ng`) and click **Add**.
3.  **Update DNS**: Vercel will provide you with **A records** or **Nameservers**.
    - Log in to your domain registrar (e.g., Whogohost).
    - Go to DNS Management.
    - Copy the values from Vercel into your registrar's dashboard.
4.  **SSL**: Vercel will automatically generate a security certificate (HTTPS) for your new domain once the DNS propagates (usually 1–24 hours).

---

## 3. Verify Favicon & Socials
- **Favicon**: I have already added the custom school emblem. After the domain is live, visit the site in an **Incognito** window to ensure the browser isn't showing a cached Vercel icon.
- **Social Sharing**: When you share the link on WhatsApp or Facebook, the school's banner image and description will automatically appear.

---

## 4. Post-Launch Checklist
- [ ] **Test Forms**: Fill out an Admissions and Careers form to ensure messages arrive on your WhatsApp.
- [ ] **Google Search**: It takes 1–2 weeks for Google to index the new domain. I have included a `sitemap.xml` to speed this up.
- [ ] **Email Setup**: You can set up `info@pureessenceschool.edu.ng` via your domain registrar or Google Workspace.
